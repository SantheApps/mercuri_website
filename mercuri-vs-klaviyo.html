The Key Difference

Klaviyo focuses on email marketing

Klaviyo is designed primarily for email marketing and advanced segmentation. If your strategy relies heavily on newsletters, promotions, and complex email flows, it may be the right fit.

The Key Difference

Mercuri focuses on conversational messaging

Mercuri is designed to help businesses communicate with customers instantly using SMS and WhatsApp, manage conversations, and automate messaging workflows. If you want to connect with customers through messaging, Mercuri is built for that.

When Klaviyo Is a Better Choice

Choose Klaviyo if email is central to your strategy

  • done You want advanced email marketing automation.
  • done You rely heavily on email campaigns and segmentation.
  • done You run a large ecommerce store focused primarily on email marketing.

Klaviyo is particularly strong for businesses that want to build complex email flows and advanced customer segments.

When Mercuri Is a Better Choice

Choose Mercuri if conversations drive growth

  • done You want to communicate with customers using SMS and WhatsApp.
  • done You need to manage two-way conversations with customers.
  • done You want to automate order notifications, reminders, and campaigns.
  • done You want messaging to run from CRMs or website platforms.
  • done You want a simpler messaging setup without complex email workflows.

Mercuri is ideal for businesses that want to build relationships through conversations instead of only sending campaigns.

Messaging vs Email

Messaging creates faster customer interaction

Email is useful for newsletters and promotions. But customers often respond faster to SMS and WhatsApp messages.

Messaging gives your team a direct way to answer questions, confirm bookings or orders, send reminders, follow up, and close sales through conversation.
