FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Mercuri pricing work?

Your total monthly cost includes a platform subscription (Free, Growth, Pro, or Enterprise), WhatsApp and SMS message charges, and $3/month per SMS phone number (if purchased). Total = Plan fee + Messages sent + Phone numbers.

How is WhatsApp pricing calculated?

WhatsApp includes Meta’s conversation fee (varies by country and type) plus Mercuri’s platform fee of $0.025 per message. This applies to template messages, campaigns, automations, and AI replies.

How is AI usage charged?

There are no AI limits. Each AI-generated reply is charged at $0.025 per message. AI continues working — you’re billed based on usage.

How is SMS pricing calculated?

SMS messages are charged per message based on destination country and carrier rates. Rates are visible in your dashboard before sending.

Do I need to buy a phone number?

SMS numbers cost $3 per month per number. WhatsApp does not require a monthly number rental.

Can I use my existing WhatsApp Business number?

Yes. You can connect your existing WhatsApp Business number to the WhatsApp Business API through Meta approval.

Are messages included in my subscription plan?

No. Your subscription gives you access to the platform features. Messaging is charged separately based on usage.

Are there any hidden fees or contracts?

No. There are no setup fees and no long-term contracts.
